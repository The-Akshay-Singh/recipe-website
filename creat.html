Meet the Creator 👋

🧑‍💻 Introduction Section

Hi, I’m Akshay Singh 👨‍🍳💻 I’m a student with a passion for both technology and creativity, and Flavorly is a reflection of that journey. This project combines my interest in web development with my love for food and simple cooking solutions.

💡 Why I Created Flavorly

Flavorly was built as a college assignment, but with a bigger vision in mind. I wanted to create a platform where:

  • Cooking feels easy and enjoyable
  • Beginners can confidently try recipes
  • People can explore food in a simple, structured way

This project is not just about recipes—it's about making everyday cooking accessible and fun.

🎯 My Vision

My goal is to turn Flavorly into more than just a project. I envision it as a platform that:

  • Helps users discover recipes based on ingredients
  • Provides smart tools like meal planners and shopping lists
  • Makes cooking less stressful and more exciting

  • Turning an idea into a working product
