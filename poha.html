Poha

🥘 Ingredients

  • 1 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 potato (optional, chopped)
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Rinse poha in water and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  3. Add peanuts and fry until crispy.
  4. Add onions, green chili, and curry leaves.
  5. Add potatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add turmeric and salt.
  7. Add poha and mix gently.
  8. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with coriander and lemon juice.
  10. Serve hot.

⏱️ Preparation Time

20 minutes

🎯 Difficulty

Easy
