Paneer Butter Masala

🥘 Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ cup cream
  • Spices (salt, red chili powder, garam masala)

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree.
  4. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add spices and mix well.
  6. Add paneer and cream.
  7. Cook for 5 minutes and serve hot.

⏱️ Preparation Time

30 minutes

🎯 Difficulty

Medium
