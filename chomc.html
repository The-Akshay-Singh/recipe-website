Chocolate Mug Cake

🥘 Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ¼ tsp baking powder

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mug.
  2. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Let it cool slightly and enjoy.

⏱️ Preparation Time

5 minutes

🎯 Difficulty

Easy
