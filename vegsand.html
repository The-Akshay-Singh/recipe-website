Veg Sandwich

🥘 Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cucumber slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Green chutney
  • Salt & pepper

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Spread butter and chutney on bread.
  2. Add vegetables evenly.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with another slice.
  5. Cut and serve.

⏱️ Preparation Time

15 minutes

🎯 Difficulty

Easy
