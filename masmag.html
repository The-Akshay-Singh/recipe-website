Masala Maggi

🥘 Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • Green chili (optional)

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add Maggi noodles and tastemaker.
  3. Add vegetables.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.

⏱️ Preparation Time

10 minutes

🎯 Difficulty

Easy
